Cultural Splash

This project aims to foster cultural communication among local German students and refugee students.


This is a project initiated by a group of diverse students. With the buddy program, the newly arrived refugees get an opportunity to learn the local German culture from German students, at the same time, the German students could also get some cultural insights from the refugee students. The project first started in Germany and later in Hong Kong.
